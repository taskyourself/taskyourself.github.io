Milestone 8

We didn't know how to go about this milestone. We could not write a contract, as we are not legally entitled to sell any products. We decided that it was enough to get some people to use the product on a daily basis.

Alpha users

After constructing the MVP, the TaskYourself team became the first user. We added tasks for our daily life and our projected and checked how the application holds up.

We indeed found issues and things that we wanted to change. For instance, the colors of the columns on the board were blue, green and pink. Teodora pointed out that it would make a lot more sense if they represented the stage of the tasks, so we changed them to red, orange and green. Andra pointed out that she would like to have a customizable profile, as most applications allow at least a personalised display. So, we changed the upper corner menu to include a Edit profile button, where users can change their display name and their profile picture. It came naturally to add a section for customizing projects.

After agreeing that the application was ready to see other users, we contacted people that left their email address on our Landing page.

Mirela Ilie, 50, Sales Representative
mirela.ilie@depo-med.ro

Mirela works at a small pharmaceutical storehouse. We were the first ones that proposed them to use a software tool for task management. TaskYourself is the first task management system she uses.

"As an employee in a small business, my schedule often ends up in being chaotic and just using an agenda, sticky notes and a calendar application is not enough to help me accomplish all my tasks and thoroughly organise them.
